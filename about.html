About us

People-centered, Solutions Driven


Achieve Solutions is a people organization “People Factory”, registered under the Companies Act in the Republic of Kenya on 18th,July 2008. We are a people-focused and result oriented organization offering affordable services with people in mind. Our services are customized to meet the needs of one person, a group or organization. We work at providing clients with the best solutions by offering structured training support, motivation, Coaching and mentorship interventions that address specific needs and achieve positive outcomes. We run workshops, hold seminars, organize conferences and facilitate business dinners, luncheons, camps and team building events that take a fresh look and feel when we are involved.

OUR MISSION

To provide motivation, mentoring, coaching, training and development services to individuals, teams, and organizations for better results and transformation.

OUR VISION

To be the most preferred training and development organization in Africa.
