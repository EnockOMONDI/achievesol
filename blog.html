JUST HOW POWERFUL IS THE MIND: FEATURING MARYHILL GIRLS HIGH SCHOOL

Progress is impossible without change and those who cannot change their minds cannot change anything. The mind is the driver of all things in the world. It could lead to success or failure. Failure is not entirely unscrupulous for it opens our eyes to things we overlook. The eye-opening phase is called mindset change and that is what Achieve Solutions is all about. Sunday of July 2019 was a beautiful day. Young minds, the future generation of leaders were congregated at Sportsview Hotel Kasarani for a session they will never forget nor regret. A session championed by Madam Hannah Njoroge, a parent’s representative of Maryhill Girls Secondary School. Great woman and a great mind.

About Us

Achieve Solutions is a people organization “People Factory”, registered under the Companies Act in the Republic of Kenya in July 2008. We are a people-focused and result oriented organization offering affordable services with people in mind. Our services are customized to meet the needs of one person, a group or organization. We work at providing clients with the best solutions by offering structured training support, motivation, Coaching and mentorship interventions that address specific needs and achieve positive outcomes. We run workshops, hold seminars, organize conferences and facilitate business dinners, luncheons, camps and team building events that take a fresh look and feel when we are involved.
