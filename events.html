About Us

Achieve Solutions is a leading organization working for a better people. The “People Factory” was registered under the Companies Act in the Republic of Kenya in 2008. We provide quality Training, Motivational talks, Coaching, Team building interventions and other personal development services that address specific needs of one person, a group or organizations of any scale. We run workshops, hold seminars, organize conferences and facilitate business dinners, luncheons and networking events that take a fresh look and feel when we are involved. We exist to bring out the best in individuals, Teams and organizations. Achieve Solutions has a team of resourceful motivators, coaches, mentors and facilitators who are ready and willing to walk with you to new heights.
