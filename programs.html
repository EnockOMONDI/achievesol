The program is designed to instil academic and moral discipline, encourage hard work, and promote education. Participants enrolled in this program will learn first-hand academic techniques, concepts and ideas that offer academic solutions to learners. The program is structured to help students in schools, colleges and universities to achieve their set goals amidst challenges. The program is designed to drive learners to achieve academic excellence and become high performing individuals who are equipped for success. We handle large and small groups and encourage small group discussions and interactive sessions with facilitators. We engage fully with students and allow feedback sessions. The program can be tailor made to suit special needs of clients. From the word achieve, our main goal is to contribute to unlocking the students’ potential by offering solutions. We remain committed to investing in students as we give them an opportunity to grow, learn, unlearn and relearn for a better tomorrow today.

The program seeks to mould world - class leaders. At Achieve Solutions, we shape and re - shape change makers and build aspiring men and women in leadership. We produce high- performing leaders who are committed to lead with excellence. This program is designed for delivering High - Performance and Impactful training on Leadership. At Achieve solutions, we do apply strategic, solution-based and astoundingly effective leadership model that produces leaders with vision for change in service.

The interactive process is designed to assist participants to access their own personal resources, capabilities and qualities to achieve defined goals. We encourage people to gain the confidence and ability to break down the barriers, that prevent them from moving forward in a positive manner.

The programme is designed to equip youth, entrepreneurs and innovators with relevant skills to start new enterprises, grow and expand businesses. The program gives participants effective business tools and a whole new set of skills in enterprise. In order to establish the areas that require attention or improvement initial assessment process with clients is conducted. Information gathered during diagnosis, coupled with relevant research, Services are tailored to tackle the specific needs of clients. After Implementation, we also recommend follow- up, which helps to reveal the extent and quality of service. We empower entrepreneurs by directly connecting them with established business professionals – who provide knowledge, experience, mentorship opportunities and networks At the end of the programme, participants develop skills and capabilities to run and grow successful enterprises.

At Achieve solutions, we create time for you, we hold your hand and encourage you through the steps. You will meet mentors with strong advice, insights, wisdom and amazing ideas that will make a huge difference in your life. We work with individuals to develop an empowered mindset and equip teams with cutting edge tools for better results and transformation.

We support individuals, teams and organizations to harness their collective energy and to develop a “Togetherness brain” approach that improves the way we supervise, manage, lead and relate as teams, and resolve conflict through a greater understanding of people dynamics and other preferred solution styles for company prosperity. We offer creative and objective facilitation to ensure effective and clear dialogue for your team. We takes activities to a different level through facilitated positive mind challenging conversations followed with relevant outdoor activities and winning concepts. The dialogue sessions are based on customized pre – event research. This is important for the analysis of the team process for purposes of identifying gaps and getting learning points for team benefit. The exercises concentrate on what hinders participants e.g. from being a cohesive high performing team and presents possible techniques for solutions. The program is designed to be flexible enough to accommodate unique needs of each client. The flexibility of content is based on clients training needs, outcomes and expectations. Flexibility of time and budget is based on your time and how much you are willing to invest.

About Us

Achieve Solutions is a leading organization working for a better people. The “People Factory” was registered under the Companies Act in the Republic of Kenya in 2008. We provide quality Training, Motivational talks, Coaching, Team building interventions and other personal development services that address specific needs of one person, a group or organizations of any scale. We run workshops, hold seminars, organize conferences and facilitate business dinners, luncheons and networking events that take a fresh look and feel when we are involved. We exist to bring out the best in individuals, Teams and organizations. Achieve Solutions has a team of resourceful motivators, coaches, mentors and facilitators who are ready and willing to walk with you to new heights.
